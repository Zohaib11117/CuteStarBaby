The Contest Phases.

The contest will run through 4 phases;
Registration Phases:
● This is the phase where contestants will register or be registered for the contest.
● The registration phase lasts for 14 days.

Voting Phase 1:

● Voting Phase 1 runs for 7 days, and it is considered to set the contest in motion.
● Contestants are to solicit votes from fans, friends, and family to be able to proceed to the next phase.
● A vote costs 50 nairas, and a minimum of 150 votes is required to proceed to Phase 2.
● Only those who get to the required vote target will proceed to Phase 2.

Voting Phase 2:

● This Phase will also run for 7 days.
● In this Phase, contestants are to solicit votes from fans, friends, and family to be able to proceed to the Winners’ Phase (Phase 3).
● A vote costs 50 nairas, and a minimum of 500 votes is required to proceed to Phase 3.
● Only those who get to the required vote target will proceed to Phase 3.

Winners’ Phase (Phase 3):

● Phase 3 is the final voting phase, which will run for only 7 days.
● Contestants are to solicit votes to become contest winners.
● A vote costs 50 nairas, a minimum of 1000 votes must be achieved to qualify for the win.
● The top three contestants with the highest number of votes will be declared winners - 1st, 2nd, and 3rd respectively.

More On How to Play

Our Selection Process

● We have adopted a 3-Phase voting process, in which voting ends by the deadline for each phase. Contestants who satisfy the requirement of both Phase 1 and Phase 2 make it to the Winners’ Phase. The contest ends by the voting deadline for the Winners’ Phase. Winners are officially announced within 72 hours after voting closes.

● The voting process will involve online voting for the contestants on our website, and each vote at all levels will cost a fixed 50 naira. Contestants who qualify for the next phase and the various winners at the end of the contest will be selected solely based on the number of votes.

Winning to the end:

Phase 1:

● All contestants are required to mobilize at least 150 votes at 50 nairas per vote and qualify for Phase 2.

Phase 2:

● All contestants are required to mobilize at least 500 votes, at 50 Naira per vote, to qualify for the final stage.

Winners’ Phase (Phase 3):

● This is the final phase of the contest. Contestants will mobilize an unlimited number of votes after hitting the minimum vote requirement of 1000 at 50 nairas per vote to come out the top.

Note
● After the Winners’ Phase ends, the top contestants with the most votes will be announced as the CUTE STAR BABIES CONTEST winners!

Prizes in the Emerald Edition;
● Gold Winner (First Place): N400,000
● Silver Winner (Second Place): N150,000
● Bronze Winner (Third Place): N70,000
● The winners must meet all the contest requirements before they are declared.
● Each contestant can vote for themselves and can be voted for as many times as possible.
● There shall be no refund of any amount of money paid to the company during the contest.
● You will have access to the scoreboard while the contest is going on so that you can monitor your progress amidst other contestants.
