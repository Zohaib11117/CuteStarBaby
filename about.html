About Us

The Cute Star Babies online events is an entertainment wing of the government registered photography brand, BlaQshot Studio. At BlaQshot Studio, we believe every child has the potential to be a star, and we are dedicated to helping them take their baby steps.

The Cute Star Babies photo contest is the first of many online activities that will be introduced to discover young talents and put them in the spotlight. Babies and Teens from ages 0 -15 that are Nigerians are eligible to participate in the photo contest with parental consent. Winners are awarded cash prizes, opportunities to represent our brand and some other add ons.

We are situated in the Federal Capital Territory, Nigeria. Our children’s entertainment events and activities are, however, fully online. As a photography brand, we exist to create lasting memories. And now, we are making it even better for your babies!

Our Mission

To provide an enjoyable experience for children while creating the largest space for self actualization.

Our Vision

Our Vision: Our vision is to be the African Child’s best friend; to build a community where children can come and discover and explore their capabilities.
